Automatic Rap Music Synthesis

Introduction

Considering the promising market of rap music in social media and the poor performance of speech-to-singing methods for rap music, our team decided to focus on rap music synthesis.

Rhythm Alignment

Rhythm, also called rap flow, is the core of rap music. Rappers enrich the fun of rap music through a variety of rhythmic variations. Different rap styles have different rhythmic characteristics, for example, triplets are often used in trap music, and Eminem’s rap flow has many quarter notes.

When labelling rhythm information, instead of annotating the timeline of lyrics, I assigned each song’s BPM and annotated fine-grained rhythmic patterns for each song in the form of MIDI files. Then, we utilized the force-alignment and PSOLA to align the rhythm of simple speech audio while maintaining its original pitch to make it have “flow”.

F1. The process of rhythm alignment.

Style Transfer

However, according to our comparison, we found that melody is actually an important part of rap flow as well. If we only align the rhythm of the speech, it would sound more like a rhythmic reading than a rap song.

To make the processed audio sound more like real rap music, we trained a modified version of the HiFi-GAN vocoder, which takes the fundamental frequency and spectrogram as input to adjust the audio pitch. We add rap styles to aligned speech by extracting pitches from existing rap audio or by using GAN to generate pitches.
